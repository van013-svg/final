Yangtze River Climate Indicators

1998 → Central + Eastern China

The 1998 Yangtze River Flood shows the escalation stage. Compared with 1887, this flood happened in a warmer world, where the atmosphere could hold more moisture and release heavier rainfall over a longer period.

tas_max: Extreme background warming variables tracking heat-induced potential.

Province notes

  • Hubei: Important middle-Yangtze region around Wuhan.
  • Hunan: Connected to Dongting Lake and flood storage pressure.
  • Anhui: Shows downstream vulnerability after upstream rainfall.
  • Jiangsu: Represents lower-basin flood risk closer to the coast.
Key pattern

The Yangtze flood shows a larger basin-wide crisis: long rainfall periods saturated the land, causing runoff to spread across multiple provinces.
